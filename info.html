HOW DOES THE GAME WORK?

  • PLAYER 1 STARTS BY CLICKING THE ROLL DICE BUTTON

  • AFTER GETTING THEIR SCORE THE PLAYER CAN CHOOSE IF
    THEY WANT TO CONTINUE TO PLAY OR CASH OUT THEIR
    POINTS WHICH WILL RESULT IN THE TURN GOING
    OVER TO THE NEXT PLAYER

  • IF THE PLAYER CHOOSES TO CONTINUE THEY
    CAN KEEP PLAYING FOR AS MANY ROLLS AS
    THEY WANT UNLESS THEY ROLL A 1

  • IF THE PLAYER ROLLS A 1 THE TURN GOES OVER
    TO THE OTHER PLAYER AND THEIR SCORE GETS SET TO 0
